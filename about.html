Responsibilities

  • C-allotrope dispersion
    Conceptualization of the project, progressing it independently.
    Project investigates the role of ionic liquids in the dissolution of carbon allotropes, a major challenge that limits the application of their exceptional properties across diverse fields. I am utilizing the enhanced sampling approach to study the free energy change during the separation process of carbon allotropes in chosen ionic liquids as a function of carbon allotrope geometry.
  • High Energy Structure Dataset (HES) Project.
    Conduct and analyze Steered MD Simulation of Protein-Ligand Complexes for the HES Dataset Project.
    Mentoring Junior Research Interns working on the High Energy Structure Project. Checking and preparing codes for the analysis along with manuscript preparation.
  • Intrinsically Disordered Protein
    Worked on Benchmark Study of Intrinsically Disordered Protein - Water Model force fields combination.
    Supported students with simulations and analysis tasks. Wrote custom analysis and visualization codes in Fortran and Python.
    Prepared manuscript.
  • Bio-catlytic Enzyme Reaction Study
    Contributed in studying the role of the BmHNL enzyme in the diastereoselective Henry reaction for producing 2-nitro-1-phenylpropan-1-ol. I worked on identifying the catalytic triad, performing docking and molecular dynamics simulations, and generating cluster-based models for DFT studies.
